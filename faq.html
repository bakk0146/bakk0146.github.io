Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
Firstly log into Web Print, upload the file you want to print, and send it to one of the printers. At the device you can release the print with your HZ card or login details.
2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
Firstly go to one of the multifunction printers at HZ, then login using your student ID or your login details, then select the 'scan' option and follow the steps on screen to scan your document. Once finished you will be able to find it online.
3. How can I buy something (e.g. IT introduction) on the HZ webshop?
Go to the Selfservice Portal on MyHZ and order whatever you want, from there on follow the steps on the screen.
4. How can you book a project space?
Book a project space here (or ask the service desk). Pick the room, date, and time, and you're set.
5. What are the instructions for parking at the HZ parking lot?
Parking at the main HZ lot is not available to students. You can park at the court, and then at JRCZ you can obtain an exit card.
