Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Firstly log into Web Print and follow the steps there, upload whatever it is that you would like to print, and send it to one of the printers. Then at the printer you can print it using your HZ card or login details.

2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Use the scan-to-email option at one of the multifunction printers here at school, on the printer enter the email address you would like to send it to, then you can open it on your laptop.

3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Go to the HZ webshop, login there, select whatever you would like to purchase, and follow the steps from there on.

4. How can you book a project space?

Go to the HZ booking system or ask the service desk, just fill in or mention the room, date, time etc and that's it!

5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

You can't. You can park at the court tho, and at the JRCZ you can get a exit card.
